Frequently asked questions

How does Adima work?

  1. Sign up
  2. Add friends
  3. Post money you can lend if you have any
  4. Request money from the borrow section
  5. Get reminders before due date
  6. Automatic repay by the due date

Can i send money abroad?

Yes definitely and we charge you as little as possible when you transfer and exchange money internationally – you can save up to 5x compared to UK high street banks.

Payment methods

  • Debit card
  • Credit card
  • Bank transfer

How much money can i borrow or lend?

You can set up your own limit when creating the account.

How do i set my preferences for notifications?

Log into your online profile and select 'View Profile Info'.

How do i change my password?

Log into your online profile from the Adima website. Then, select “View Profile Info”, and click the edit icon next to your password.
